ABOUT ME

A Vtuber from India

SoulOfSwords, also known as SOS, is a Babiniku VTuber from India. As a Babiniku, SoulOfSwords represents a gender-fluid persona, playing around with identity and adopting female avatars despite being male in real life. This avatar journey reflects an ongoing process of finding the "perfect model", a challenge that many VTubers face in refining their appearance and persona over time.

Their backstory or lore centers on the idea of being a soul trapped in a sword, a curse only breakable by someone who can wield them. This unique narrative ties into the channel's thematic focus on swords and the versatility of their models, where they adapt based on viewers' desires—highlighting a deep connection between the VTuber and their audience.
